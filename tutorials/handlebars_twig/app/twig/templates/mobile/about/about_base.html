About

The IF List ‒ The Imagine Film List ‒ is a free online database and platform for movie ideas based on books, comics, real events and other sources. The IF List allows anyone, inside or outside the entertainment industry, to discover and propose stories for film and TV, share their dream casts, and gain popular support for the ideas they want to become reality.

THE IF LIST IS A RESOURCE FOR:

Authors ‒ to promote their work to fans and to film and TV producers

Actors ‒ to market themselves to fans and campaign for dream roles

Filmmakers ‒ to build support for their projects and ideas

Producers ‒ to discover new stories and gauge audience demand

Casting Directors ‒ to find the right talent for the roles they need to fill
